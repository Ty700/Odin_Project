Hamburger Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1 Large Egg
  2. 1/2 tsp Salt
  3. 1/2 tsp pepper
  4. 1lb Groun

    Directions

    1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
    2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
    3. Add gorund beef and bread crumbs; mix until well blended.
    4. Form into 4 - 3/4in thick patties
    5. Place patties on the preheated gril.
    6. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
