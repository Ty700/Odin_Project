Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. Butter
  2. 1 Small Onion (chopped)
  3. 1 Bellpepper (chopped)
  4. 1 - 1.5lb Hamburger Meat
  5. 2 tbs Garlic Powder
  6. 3 tbs Butter
  7. 1 tsp Salt
  8. 1 tsp pepper
  9. 2 15 Ounce Tomato Saunce
  10. 1lb Spaghetti Noodles

Directions

  1. On medium heat, melt the butter and sautee the onion and bell peppers.
  2. Add the hamburger meat and cook untl meat is well done.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  4. Cook noodles as directed.
  5. Mix the sauce and noodles.
